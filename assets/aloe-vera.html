ALOE-VERA GRUEL INITIAL TREATMENT IN HIV INFECTION

Human Immunodeficiency virus (HIV) - a lentivirus that causes HIV infection and Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome.It is a condition in humans in which progressive failure of the immune system allows life threatening infections and cancers to thrive

(Ram and Ellen, 2004).

HIV infection leads to low levels of CD4+ T cells through apoptosis of uninfected bystander cells, direct viral killing of infected cells, and killing of infected CD4+ T cells by CD8 cytotoxic lymphocytes that recognize infected cells.

(Garg et al., 2012)

MODE OF TRANSMISSION OF HIV INFECTION

  • Direct transfer of genital, rectal or oral fluids through sexual intercourse
  • Sharing of blood-contaminated needles
  • Childbirth
  • Breastfeeding
  • Medical procedures (e.g. transfusions, exposure to contaminated instruments)

MODE OF TRANSMISSION OF HIV INFECTION

  • HIV Infection may initially be asymptomatic or cause acute retroviral syndrome.
  • Fever with intermittent sweats
  • Diarrhoea
  • Fatigue
  • Lymphadenopathy
  • Herpes zoster
  • White plaques due to oral candidiasis
